Pikmin is if not one of the best Nintendo series ever for me, even above Mario, It is a very personal game to me that I feel always deserves more love than it gets, Because it could of easily fallen into the same hole as another game, Chibi-robo, left as a character always stuck in cheaply made games sold in Walmarts, never given another chance again.

But Fortunetly, Nintendo seemed to think it did well enough to recive a sequal, then a third game for the Wii U, which may have been the first game for many! then nintendo released Pikmin 4, which i felt like it was Pikmin at its peak, for once people who didnt know what Pikmin were, were willing to give this franchise a chance!

Hence why this Page now exists, Whenever i am able, i will be writing about all the possible Pikmin Games that have released (and are still accesable) It will take awhile, but i want to make sure i put as much effort into writing these as possible, because i care too much about this game with carrots with faces :)
