Pikmin 2 is one of (in my opinion) more of the fun Gamecube era Pikmin games, as they get rid of the time limit and let you play at your own pace.

The reason you dont have to rush? your life support actually works this time!, unfortunetly you are sent back to the planet PNF-404 to find treasure, as your Boss of Hocotate Freights has fallen into debt after all his Golden Pikpik Carrots were eaten.

So now its up to you, and your new partner Louie (who always seems hungry...) to get your boss out of his debt!

I love Pikmin 2 simply because it cements what the Pikmin's series energy is, its silly, but still has alot of life and death elements, and i love how all the "treasures" are just objects we regularly use, like a battery or a bar of soap! and if you are playing the older GC/Wii versions of the game, they are all known brands. (Pikmin 1+2 for Switch replaces them with generic non real brands, for licencing reasons i think)

And the new "Dungeon" gameplay mechanic, where its just a level of enemies and treasures is neat, becuase time actually freezes when you are down in one, however, this is the kind of Pikmin game where you shouldnt get too attached to them.... This is possibly the most heart breaking Pikmin game, since you are pretty much garenteed to kill a whole group of Pikmin... may their sacrifices never be in vain...
