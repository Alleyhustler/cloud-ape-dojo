What is Tomodachi Life?

Tomodachi Life is a game created by Nintendo For the 3DS, and it is half Life Sim and half Virtual pet!, as you take care of your Mii's that you have on your device (or even ones from others with QR Codes!) and they all live in an apartment together!, they can develop friendships, fall in love, have kids, but they can also fall out or have arguments... So you the player (Or your first characters "Lookalike") act as a god, taking care of your people, giving them advice, and gifts!

Who can you add?

If you can somehow make it on the Mii Maker on the 3DS, or find a QR code online, basically anyone you want!, you can add yourself, family or friends, your favorate characters like Hatsune Miku, or even some weird creatons like twoface or even, somehow, a burger (i am not kidding LMAO)
