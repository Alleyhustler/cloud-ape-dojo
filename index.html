From every corner of the chain, the Apes gathered — not to fight, but to breathe. The Dojo was formed from the first banana tree that sprouted on Solana, grown from a seed of patience and watered with chill. The Dojo is not a promise of riches, but a path of clarity. The market is the wind — unpredictable, but never your enemy. A true Ape floats with it. Ape not to win. Ape to become still.
